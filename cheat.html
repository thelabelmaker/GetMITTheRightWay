How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same college touch, they merge into one!
Note: The order of colleges is not intended to be a ranking (but Caltech is the best), but a rough east-to-west geographical path to Caltech.

Originally created by Mitchell Gu. Inspired by Get Caltech by Naveen Arun. Based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. and 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer. Modified by Ethan L.
